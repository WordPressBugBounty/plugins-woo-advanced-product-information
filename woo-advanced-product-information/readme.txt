Makes your product page informative with additional info, such as: Review, Stock, Sales, Countdown, Coupon, Social Proof, Rank and more.

== Description ==

Display more intuitive information of products such as sale countdown, sale badges, who recently bought products, rank of products in their categories, available payment methods...

>[Try demo](https://new2new.com/?item=woo-advanced-product-information "Advanced Product Information for WooCommerce") | [Documents](https://docs.villatheme.com/?item=woo-advanced-product-information "Documents") | [Facebook group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/villatheme "VillaTheme")

### Important Notice:

- Plugin works based on WooCommerce 7.x plugin.

- It is released on WordPress.org and you can use plugin as free to build themes for sale.

### FEATURES:

&#9658; **Review Rating:** A better reviews system to tell customers how buyers were satisfied with your products: Be the first to review this product - Display message when there is no review on a product page, 100% of buyers are satisfied with this product - Display message when all reviews are 5-stars reviews

&#9658; **Product quantity:** Display how many quantities of the product left in your store.

&#9658; **Shipping options:** Show shipping availability on the product page.

&#9658; **Sale Saves:** Display how much money customer can save if buying during discount time.

&#9658; **Sale Countdown:** Show a sale countdown timer for on-sale products. There's an option to loop countdown timer for on-sale products without scheduled time.

&#9658; **Sales count:** Display message how many orders of the product in a chosen time. The order quantity can be got from WooCommerce or create virtually.

&#9658; **Rank in Category:** Display sale-rank of the product in categories with 3 available shortcodes {rank} {category} {time}.

&#9658; **Payment Methods:** Available payment methods will be displayed with icons or images.

&#9658; **Social Proof:** Display how many customers have visited the product page.

&#9658; **Coupon:** Display a coupon on the product page with full information: discount amount, expiry date.

&#9658; **Recent orders:** Who recently bought a product? This can be show to your customer

&#9658; **Conditions:** For each of the above features, you can set which products, categories to be included or excluded

&#9658; **Design:** All the advanced information is fully customizable with the message text, text color, text background color.

1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
